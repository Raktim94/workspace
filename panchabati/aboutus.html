    Who We Are

    Escape to Nature. Embrace the Silence.

    Welcome to Panchabati Eco Homestay. We are more than just a place to sleep; we are a sanctuary located in the heart of the Jungle Mahal.

    Situated in the picturesque village of Dhoba Dhobin, our homestay was built with a simple vision: to offer travelers a place where the rustic charm of Bengal meets modern comfort. Whether you want to wake up to birdsong or watch the lake glow at sunset, we are your home away from home.

    "We don't just host guests; we welcome family. Come, slow down, and find your rhythm with nature."
