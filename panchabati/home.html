    Introduction

    A Hidden Gem in Jhargram

    Welcome to Panchabati Eco Homestay, where the rustic charm of Bengal meets modern comfort. Located in the picturesque village of Dhoba Dhobin, just 4.5 km from Jhargram Station, our property offers a unique "Lake View" experience that you won't find anywhere else.

    Imagine waking up to the gentle breeze of the Panchabati Mahato Sarobar, sipping your morning tea while watching the sunrise over lush paddy fields, and spending your evenings strolling across the viral "Bamboo Bridge." Whether you are a couple seeking a romantic getaway, a family looking for a safe vacation, or a group of friends ready for adventure, we provide the perfect base camp for your Jungle Mahal exploration.
