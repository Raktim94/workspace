    About Swapnabhumi 

    Swapnabhumi is a landmark residential township project spread across 500+ acres near the IT hub of Newtown, Kolkata. Designed as a gated community with tree-lined avenues and ample open space, Swapnabhumi offers finished plot delivery (land filling, roads, underground utilities), complete documentation and robust infrastructure — making it ideal for families, professionals and investors seeking long-term value.

    Key facts:

    • 500+ acres master-planned township
    • 1200+ plots — documentation completed
    • 40% green & open spaces
    • Underground drainage, street lighting & underground electricity
    • Wide internal roads (22–50 ft blacktop).
